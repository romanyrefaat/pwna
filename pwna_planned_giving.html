Planned Giving

Did you know it is possible to assist Native Americans without it costing you anything during your lifetime?

PWNA offers a variety of ways that you can donate to a great cause and help to ensure a brighter future for Native Americans living on remote, isolated and impoverished reservations in the Northern Plains and Southwest — while achieving your financial goals.

  • Bequests in your Will or Trust
  • Life Insurance Policy Donation
  • Retirement Plan Donation
  • Donor-Advised Funds
  • Donation of stock
  • Charitable Gift Annuities

If you have any questions regarding planned giving, please contact Suzi Smith in our Planned Giving Department at (877) 853-6474. You are also encouraged to consult with your legal and financial advisors when considering a planned gift.
