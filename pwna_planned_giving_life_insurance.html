Life Insurance Policy Donation

Your life insurance policy is usually an untapped and forgotten part of your financial planning. Yet it is a simple way to ensure the continued work of [[S80:sitename]].

There are many ways that your life insurance policy can benefit Native American people.

Options to donate your policy:
  • You can name [[S80:sitename]] as a beneficiary in your current life insurance policy.
  • You can donate your paid-up policy directly to [[S80:sitename]].
  • You can start a new policy, making [[S80:sitename]] the owner and beneficiary.
Benefits for you:
  • It does not affect you financially during your lifetime.
  • There are potential income tax deductions if you transfer the policy to [[S80:prefix]].
  • Estate taxes can be reduced if you name [[S80:prefix]] as the beneficiary.
