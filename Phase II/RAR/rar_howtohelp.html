How to Help

The Reservation Animal Rescue (RAR) Program depends on the generosity of private donors to fund our services that improve the quality of life for stranded and orphaned animals on Native American reservations.

Thanks to the kindness of people just like you and through dedicated Program Partners, RAR touches the lives of thousands dogs and cats on reservations throughout the Northern Plains and Southwest.

For more information, please call Donor Relations at (866) 311-9635.
