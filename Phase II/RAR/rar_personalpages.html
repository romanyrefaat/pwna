Sage Pages

Native Americans have long honored the wisdom animals provide for our lives. As an animal-lover, you have experienced this for yourself.

RAR Sage Pages allow you to create and personalize your own web page to honor the animal wisdom in your life, sharing their pictures and stories — have fun with it!

In their honor, you can also help raise funds for animals needing assistance on reservations. Check out other pages as examples or learn more about traditional Native American animal wisdom.

Create your own Sage Page, share your favorite furry friend with the world, and set up a fund to help rez animals! Edit your Sage Page via your own Champion Center. You can also share your page via emails with all your friends. Search for other Sage Pages to see examples, to vote, or to donate to a special friend’s fund. Learn more about Native American animal wisdom and legends.
