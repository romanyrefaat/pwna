Make a Donation

Your generous donations allow us to work with our Program Partners to provide vital support for thousands of Native American children, Elders, and families living in reservation communities throughout the Northern Plains and Southwestern states.

Your online donation is the most cost-efficient method of giving, allowing us to use more funds to help those we serve.
