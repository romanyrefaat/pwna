How to Help

The American Indian Education Fund (AIEF) Program depends on the generosity of private donors to fund our services that improve the quality of life for Native American students address persistent challenges and strengthen their resources for greater impact.

Thanks to the kindness of people just like you and through dedicated Program Partners, AIEF touches the lives of thousands students throughout the Northern Plains and Southwest.

For more information, please call Donor Relations at (800) 881-8694.
