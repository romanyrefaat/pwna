Did you know that it is possible to assist animals living on Native American reservations without it costing you anything during your lifetime?

You can help by joining The Heritage Circle which enables you to donate using any of our planned giving options! The Rescue Operation for Animals of the Reservation Program offers a variety of ways that you can donate to a great cause while achieving your financial goals.

“I received the very lovely framed certificate. It really wasn't necessary as I give without expecting anything in return. Since I haven't decided where to display it yet, it is currently sitting in a place of honor, on my kitchen island where I can look at it multiple times during the day. When someone comes to visit me, they will also see it and then I will tell them just how important it is to donate."
